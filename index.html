Reccomended Movies

Dune: Part One

"Dune: Part One" (2021) is an American sci-fi epic directed by Denis Villeneuve. Serving as the first installment of a two-part adaptation of Frank Herbert's 1965 novel, the film unfolds in the distant future. It follows Paul Atreides and his noble House Atreides as they become embroiled in a war for control of the harsh desert planet Arrakis.

Fantastic Beasts: The Secrets of Dumbledore

Fantastic Beasts: The Secrets of Dumbledore (2022) is a fantasy film directed by David Yates, written by J.K. Rowling and Steve Kloves. It's the sequel to "Fantastic Beasts: The Crimes of Grindelwald" (2018), the third installment in the Fantastic Beasts series, and the eleventh in the Wizarding World franchise.

Badland Hunters

Badland Hunters" (Korean: 황야) is a 2024 South Korean dystopian action film, directed by Heo Myung-haeng in his debut. The film is a sequel to Concrete Utopia, exploring the post-earthquake transformation of Seoul into an apocalyptic wasteland, where law, order, and civilization have collapsed.
